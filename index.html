Who We Are

The Mira Costa Cybersecurity Club is dedicated to educating and promoting cybersecurity and technology through competitions and off-season activities. We welcome students of all skill levels who are interested in learning about information security, ethical hacking, and digital forensics.

Join Our Club

When: Wednesdays at lunch

Where: Room 112

What to expect: Learn about cybersecurity, participate in competitions, and connect with fellow tech enthusiasts!

No prior experience necessary - just bring your curiosity and enthusiasm for technology and security!
