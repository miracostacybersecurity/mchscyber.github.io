About Us

Who We Are

The Mira Costa Cybersecurity Club is dedicated to educating and promoting cybersecurity and technology through competitions and off-season activities.

We welcome students of all skill levels who are interested in learning about information security, ethical hacking, and digital forensics.

What We Do

  • Weekly Presentations & Workshops: Learn about various cybersecurity topics through hands-on activities and expert presentations
  • Competitions: Compete in national events like CyberPatriot, National Cyber League, and various CTF challenges
  • Industry Events: Attend cybersecurity conferences and events such as DEFCON and Cyberstrike by INL
  • Community: Connect with fellow students passionate about technology and security
