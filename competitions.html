CyberPatriot

Mandatory, Fall

This event, created by the Air Force Association, is the foundation of our club. Teams compete for the top placement within their state and region, and the top teams in the nation earn all-expenses-paid trips to Baltimore, MD for the National Finals Competition. Learn more at uscyberpatriot.org

National Cyber League

Optional, Fall and Spring

The National Cyber League (NCL) is a virtual training ground for high school and college students to develop, practice and validate cybersecurity skills using next-generation simulation technology. Our team achieved Diamond rank!

How CyberPatriot Works

Each team of 5-6 members is assigned an image (virtual computer) that has multiple flaws and deficiencies. The team must try to secure as many flaws to the system as they can. For each flaw secured, points are added depending on the level of difficulty.

The competition takes place during over (non consecutive) weekends. Each team has 6 consecutive hours on one day of the weekend to secure as many flaws as possible on the virtual machines.

At the end of the six hours, points are totaled up and the team is ranked based on those of the rest of the United States. Based on the team's score in the first two rounds, the team is placed into a tier – Platinum, Gold, or Silver.
